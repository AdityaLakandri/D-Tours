Glenary's Restaurant

(Indian,Chinese,Continental)

This restaurantis over 100 years old and has been running since the old colonial days.It's located on the busy Nehru Road. The ground floor is where the well known Glenary's Bakery & cafe is located and you will find The Buzz, a modern pub at the basement.The restaurant has great views of mountains and valleys on one side while the other side overlooks the Nehru road. Some of the favourites here are Tandoor specials(earthen oven baked),sizzlers and chinese dishes. The restaurant stocks nice selection of alcoholic beverages including wine. While the cafe & bakery offers nice breakfast and is open for lunch and dinner.

Shangri-La

(Indian,Chinese,tibetan

As you walk few steps from Glenary's towards the Chowrasta Mall, Shangri-La Restaurant will be on your right. It is actually part of a two storied old heritage building which is run as hotel with only three room upstairs, and the restaurant is in the ground floor. The restaurant is nice with wooden decor,arches and great interiors. You will get all kinds of food here including Chinese, North Indian style,Tandoor items as well as sea food.There is also anice bar at the end of the restaurant.

Keventer's

(English Breakfast & Snacks

For us, a Darjeeling vacation is nevr complete without a breakfast at the Keventer's.If you like to go for traditional English breakfast with items like great sandwiches, chicken salami & meat loafs, or pork sausages with eggs followed bya a pot of perfect Darjeelingtea ,this isthe place to go. Located right at the beginning of Nehru Road that leads towards the mall, this strange looking narrow building has two floor. The ground flooris a bakery. Climb up the stairs to enter the small cozy indoor area or just walk across to come out the open terrace that has Kachenjunga and other lovely views around.

Park Restaurant

Thai Cuisine & Others

Park is also one of our old time favourites. Located on Laden la Road and opposite to the State Bank of India building,this restaurant offers great Thai cuisine.As the owner Deep says, once you have Thai dishes , you won't like to have Chinese items as they would taste bland. However the restaurant does offer a far wider range of menus including North Indian and Chinese Dishes.

Lunar Restaurant

(Only Vegetarian, North & South Indian/Chinese)

This vegetarian restaurant is located on Gandhi road next to the taxi stand and tucked between the Dekevas restaurant at the ground floor and the Dekeling hotel above. This restaurant have a fairly ong menu of vegetarian dishes that include both north and south Indian items, Chinese and snack like sandwiches.

Kunga

(Chinese and Tibetan)

It's a small eatery with only handful of tables but remains full most times.You can see many foriegn tourists here relishing on hot Chinese and Tibetan food. The food is not only good,the menu is quite extensive and portions are large. It's run by a local family. Kunga is locatedon Gandhi road right at the upper clubside taxi stand(near Planter's Club).
