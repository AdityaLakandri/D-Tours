The Queen Of Hills

Darjeeling, one of the most famous destinations in north east tourism is dream destinations for all tourists who wish to spend same tranquil time amidst nature. Enveloped in captivating natural beauty, Darjeeling has given the tag of "The Quuen Of Hills." Darjeeling is famous for its tea it grows and the great view of Kanchejunga range mountains that it offers. it is also known for its richness in cultural & natural heritage and the famous toy train that has been declared in UN heritage.
